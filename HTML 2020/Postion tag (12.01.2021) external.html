Web services are categorised into two types: SOAP and REST. Typically SOAP and REST are the methods used to call the web services. There are several differences between SOAP and REST. Firstly SOAP relies on XML to assist the services while REST can support various formats such as HTML, XML, JSON, etc. Another significant difference is that SOAP is a protocol.
